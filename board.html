About the Board

The Board is composed of alumni from recent cohorts who volunteer their time to keep the program running and to help shape what the next cohort experiences.

They review applications, lead monthly discussions, coordinate events and social gatherings, manage the program's voice on social media, and shepherd an alumni community that now spans more than 175 emerging Africa-focused policy professionals.

Interested in serving on a future board?

Board roles rotate annually and are open to alumni of the program. Reach out if you'd like to learn more about the commitment and timeline.
